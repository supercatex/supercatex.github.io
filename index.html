Artificial Intelligence in Home Service Robots

Our group was formed in early 2019. By chance, we met Professor Chen, and everyone agreed that artificial intelligence and robotics would be major trends in future development. With the research and development of neural networks, AI will be able to perform more complex tasks in the future, becoming increasingly integrated into our daily lives and work.

In technology education, we also need to keep up with the times, giving students the opportunity to engage with new technologies and broaden their horizons. Therefore, our group focuses on AI and robotics, providing a platform for students to explore the relationship between technology and our lives. Through hands-on projects, they can build their own robots and integrate AI to complete various tasks.

Artificial intelligence (AI) is increasingly being integrated into home service robots to enhance their functionality and efficiency. These robots are designed to assist with daily household tasks, improving convenience and quality of life.
